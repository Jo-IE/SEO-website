About Us

SEO International is a world class company delivering cutting edge energy solutions, Electricity Generation and Distribution, Renewable Energy, Oil and Gas Installation Systems, Offshore Services and Support, Utility Management Systems, Infrastructure Development and Management, Piling and Port Development, Fabrication, Refinery EPC , and allied services.

Company Overview

SEO International Ltd is a Limited liability Company which was incorporated in 1995 and consists of Principal Officers with combined industry experience of over 150 years.

The company was formed to take advantage of the perceived weaknesses, inadequacies and paucity of efficient and reliable service delivery in our core areas of specialization while upholding the best tenets of professionalism, precision and client satisfaction.

With our dynamism and use of modern technology, financial resources and firm support from our Team Members and partners worldwide, we are undoubtedly poised to deliver on our promises to our numerous clients Worldwide.

Our Mission

The mission of SEO International Limited is to provide top rate, world-class services in our core areas of specialization particularly in emerging global markets where demand is abundant but quality service is scarce . The company seeks to continually provide these services in the most timely manner and with an on-going comprehensive quality control program to provide full customer satisfaction. Each service is seen as an agreement not between a business and its clients, but between partners working to create close and mutually beneficial long-term relationships.

Our Progress

Over the years we have learned from every project and aspire to achieve steady improvement and growth.

Our unparalleled experience in Computer Aided Designs, Engineering and Instrumentation Systems, combined with the exploit of Artificial Intelligence, avails us the insight and proficiency needed to operate complex Energy Systems, Oil and Gas Installation Systems and Remote Control Systems for various Applications.

Our broad and deep knowledge of infrastructure management systems for utilities puts us in the ideal position to drive the greatest opportunity in today’s energy market: the Smart Grid.

We have the expertise to turn the promise of the Smart Grid technology in the electric transmission and distribution grid networks, into reality for utilities in Cities in emerging economies worldwide.
